Dev Academy Reflections

Sprint 1.3

4th September 2018

The EDA experience is clearly quite different from the typical university or school learning expereince. It's values driven, inclusive, and pracitcal. So far I am really enjoying it.

My impression is that is a very inclusive learning environment. Although it will be challenging and tough at times, if we are open minded, kind, and put in the effort, we will be supported and succeed.

I think I will enjoy having the opportunity to learn completely new concepts, and I will enjoy getting to know people from all sorts of different backgrounds. However, I do tend to get frustrated easily, I tend to be quite slow (particularly when it comes to practical learning) and I'm impatient! I quite aware this isn't the best recipe for success so I will need to be very aware of myself, and not stress if I'm behind others.

I'm both excited and nervous! I am a complete beginner, and my top subjects are History and English! So I am really getting out of my comfort zone doing something so practical like this.
